Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 February 2026

Atab (Pty) Ltd (“Atab”, “we”, “us”) is a South African data consulting and analytics company. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) and, where applicable, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process limited personal information when providing consulting services, building data systems, or integrating with third-party platforms. This may include:

  • Names and business contact details
  • Professional or employment-related information
  • System identifiers required for API integrations
  • Usage or diagnostic data related to systems we develop or operate

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information unless explicitly required by a client and governed by a written agreement.

2. How We Use Information

Personal information is processed only for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Delivering agreed consulting or technical services
  • Integrating and operating third-party systems on behalf of clients
  • Improving system reliability, security, and performance
  • Meeting legal or regulatory obligations

We do not sell personal information.

3. Data Sources

Data processed by Atab typically originates from:

  • Our clients and their authorised systems
  • Third-party platforms explicitly approved by clients
  • Public or licensed data sources

4. Data Sharing

We may share personal information only:

  • With clients who control the data
  • With service providers acting under our instruction
  • Where required by law

All sharing is limited to what is necessary for the stated purpose.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational safeguards to protect personal information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

6. Data Retention

Personal information is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, unless a longer retention period is required by law or contract.

  • Access your personal information
